Welcome to Government Polytechnic Lucknow

Government Polytechnic Lucknow of Engineering & Technology is managed by Government Polytechnic Lucknow Educational Trust founded in the year 2007 and registered under the Societies Registration Act of 1860 with an aim of providing quality technical education.


 The Institute is affiliated to AKTU, Lucknow and approved by AICTE,.Ministry of HRD, Govt of India.
